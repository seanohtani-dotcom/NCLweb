Things to Know About Philippines Cruises

Best Time to Cruise

November to April offers the best weather with dry conditions and calm seas. This is peak season for Philippines NCL cruises.

What to Pack

  • Lightweight, breathable clothing
  • Reef-safe sunscreen
  • Snorkeling gear (optional)
  • Waterproof camera
  • Light rain jacket
  • Comfortable walking shoes

Currency & Tipping

Philippine Peso (PHP) is the local currency. USD is widely accepted. Tipping is appreciated but not mandatory on Philippines NCL cruises.

Health & Safety

No special vaccinations required. Drink bottled water and use reef-safe sunscreen to protect marine life. Philippines NCL maintains high safety standards.

Cultural Etiquette

Filipinos are known for their hospitality. Learn basic Tagalog phrases, respect local customs, and always smile - it's the Filipino way!
