    ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

    What documents do I need for my cruise?

    You'll need a valid passport with at least 6 months validity, booking confirmation, and completed health declaration form. Additional visas may be required depending on your nationality.

    When should I arrive at the port?

    We recommend arriving at the port 2-3 hours before departure time. This allows sufficient time for check-in, security screening, and boarding procedures.

    What's included in my cruise fare?

    Your cruise fare includes accommodation, all meals at main dining venues, entertainment shows, pool and fitness facilities, and transportation between ports.

    Can I cancel or modify my booking?

    Yes, you can cancel or modify your booking subject to our cancellation policy. Fees may apply depending on how close to departure you make changes.

    What should I pack for my cruise?

    Pack lightweight, breathable clothing, swimwear, comfortable walking shoes, sunscreen, hat, and any personal medications. Formal attire is optional for special dinners.

    Is Wi-Fi available on board?

    Yes, Wi-Fi packages are available for purchase. Basic packages include email and messaging, while premium packages offer full internet access.
